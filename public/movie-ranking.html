The Best Movies That Doesn't Exist

My top 3 fictional movies.

Movie #1701

Yes, a generically numbered movie. It shows numbers and an infinite tunnel of bronze frames. Critics were extremely mixed on this, from "complete masterpiece" to "absurdly boring trash".

Jack's Stalk

Even after watching the movie, the viewers question what the title is trying to imply when the movie itself was showing something completely unrelated. And no, it was rated E for everyone.

Orange Blue Magenta

A parody on "The Powerpuff Girls", but it shows three hipsters raiding farms in style. This movie is two hours long; but of the three raids in this movie, the second one lasted for an hour and twenty minutes.
